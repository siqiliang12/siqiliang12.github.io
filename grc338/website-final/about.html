Welcome to Matcha & Moka—where Asian tradition meets modern coffee culture. Founded by Jane and Akiro, the shop blends Jane’s Japanese Matcha expertise with Akiro’s rich Vietnamese coffee heritage to create a unique, inviting space.

At Matcha & Moka, every sip reflects our heritage and love for quality. From perfectly whisked Matcha Lattes to bold Moka brews, our menu honors the flavors and traditions that define us.

We value sustainability and community, sourcing our tea and coffee from family-owned farms that honor traditional, eco-friendly practices—giving back to the roots that inspire us.
